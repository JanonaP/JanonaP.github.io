The "Seven" Kingdoms

Although the characters repeatedly state that there are seven kingdoms in Westeros, there are actually nine kingdoms:

  1. The North
  2. The Vale
  3. The Iron Islands
  4. The Riverlands
  5. The Westerlands
  6. The Stormlands
  7. The Reach
  8. The Crownlands
  9. Dorne

Catelyn Stark wasn't supposed to die.

After the Red Wedding, Catelyn Stark was supposed to revive as Lady Stoneheart. She cannot speak, but in the books, she seeks revenge against those who arranged the Red Wedding.

Daenerys's Dragons

Daenerys's dragons, Drogon, Rhaegal, and Viserion, are small compared to her ancestors' dragons. Balerion was the largest dragon, whose rider was Aegon the Conqueror.
